About the Duo

Two Artists.
One Sound.

Deni Bonet & Chris Flynn are a dynamic musical duo whose sound refuses to stay in one lane — and that's exactly the point.

Drawing from a rich palette of folk, rock, jazz, and everything in between, their music is a joyful collision of virtuosity and spontaneity. Deni's signature violin weaves melodies that range from tender to fierce, while Chris's guitar lays down grooves as likely to move you to tears as to pull you onto the dance floor.

Together, they bring an energy that is electric, unpredictable, and entirely their own — whether performing intimate venues or packed festival stages.
